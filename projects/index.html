Portfolio Analysis and Sustainable Fund Comparison

[Link to Draft PDF]

Status: Draft / Unsubmitted   |   Field: Portfolio Analysis

Collaborated equally with a team of four on a comprehensive finance coursework project as part of the 'Capital Market Analysis' module at the University of Nottingham. This project created a custom US stock portfolio (Oct. 2023 - Sep. 2024) and evaluated it against the Russell 1000 Growth Index and the BGF US Growth Fund. The decisions were informed based upon academic literature on the efficient market hypothesis and the debate between 'Active versus Passive Investing'. In addition, 4 sustainable US equity funds were analysed, the: Liontrust GF Sustainable Future US Growth Fund, FTGF ClearBridge US Sustainability Leaders Fund, Fidelity Sustainable US Equity Fund and the J.P. Morgan US Sustainable Equity Fund.

Risk-Based Portfolio Optimization with FTSE 100 Stocks (2019–2020)

[Link to Draft PDF]

Status: Draft / Unsubmitted   |   Field: Portfolio Optimisation

Inspired by Daniel P. Palomar in his book titled: "Portfolio Optimization: Theory and Application". This project compares the performance of five risk-based portfolio optimization strategies—Global Minimum Variance Portfolio (GMVP), Inverse Volatility Portfolio (IVolP), Risk-Parity Portfolio (RPP), Most Diversified Portfolio (MDivP), and Maximum Decorrelation Portfolio (MDecP)—using eight stocks from the FTSE 100 index over the period of 2019–2020.
