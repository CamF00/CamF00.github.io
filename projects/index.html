Diversification Optimisation with Low-Correlation Alternative Assets

[Link to Draft PDF]

Status: Work In Progress [WIP]   |   Field: Portfolio Optimisation

To construct a diversified investment portfolio that maximizes risk-adjusted returns (e.g., Sharpe Ratio) by incorporating low-correlation alternative assets, minimizing portfolio volatility while preserving expected returns. The project emphasizes alternative assets such as real estate, commodities, and cryptocurrencies, which typically exhibit low or negative correlations (generally <0.4) with traditional assets like equities and bonds.

Risk-Based Portfolio Optimization with FTSE 100 Stocks (2019–2020)

[Link to Draft PDF]

Status: Work In Progress [WIP]   |   Field: Portfolio Optimization

Inspired by Daniel P. Palomar in his book titled: "Portfolio Optimization: Theory and Application". This project compares the performance of five risk-based portfolio optimization strategies—Global Minimum Variance Portfolio (GMVP), Inverse Volatility Portfolio (IVolP), Risk-Parity Portfolio (RPP), Most Diversified Portfolio (MDivP), and Maximum Decorrelation Portfolio (MDecP)—using eight stocks from the FTSE 100 index over the period of 2019–2020.
